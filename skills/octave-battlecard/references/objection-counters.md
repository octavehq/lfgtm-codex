# Mode: Objection Counters

Objection titles describe the **risk or situation**, never a quote in the prospect's voice — the actual line goes in "You'll hear." Responses are **points the rep adapts on the call**, not word-for-word rebuttals to recite.

```
OBJECTION COUNTERS: vs [Competitor]
====================================

PRICING
-------

They anchor on [Competitor]'s lower sticker price
  You'll hear: "[Competitor] is cheaper"
  Response: [TCO, hidden-cost, and value points to adapt]
  Proof: [customer metric or quote]

Budget is committed to the incumbent
  You'll hear: "We don't have budget to switch"
  Response: [migration support, ROI-timeline points]
  Proof: [switching-ROI proof point]

---

FEATURE / TECHNICAL
-------------------

They lean on a feature [Competitor] markets
  You'll hear: "[Competitor] has [Feature X]"
  Response: [our approach, why ours is better or different]
  Proof: [evidence]

Integration concern
  You'll hear: "[Competitor] integrates with [Tool Y]"
  Response: [our integrations / API points]
  Proof: [evidence]

---

RELATIONSHIP / INERTIA
----------------------

Long-tenured on the incumbent
  You'll hear: "We've been with [Competitor] for years"
  Response: [switching cost vs. opportunity cost]
  Proof: [long-time customer who finally switched]

Team familiarity
  You'll hear: "Our team knows [Competitor]"
  Response: [onboarding experience, time to value]
  Proof: [evidence]

---

RISK
----

Perceived switching risk
  You'll hear: "Switching is too risky"
  Response: [migration support, phased approach]
  Proof: [smooth migration story]

---

FROM REAL CONVERSATIONS
-----------------------
[If conversation data available, cite actual objections heard and what worked]
• "[Real objection from call]" — Handled by: [rep / approach] — Outcome: [won/lost]

---

Sources: [Competitor entity, conversation findings, deal outcomes]
```
